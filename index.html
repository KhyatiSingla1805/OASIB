Priyanka Chopra

About

Priyanka Chopra is a very well known name in the Indian film industry. Not only in Indian but around the world she is an inspiring figure. With her hard work in acting, singing and her spectrum of philanthropic activities, she has emerged as one of the well known names in the Indian film industry. She is born to physician parents and is born in Jamshedpur which is a small town in the Indian state of Jharkhand. She had to move to different places to complete her school because of her parent's job. And, at the age of 13 years, her parents sent her to USA for studies, where she also took up some volunteering work in singing and dancing apart from taking up her formal studies. At that time, she desired to become a psychologist or an engineer instead of becoming an artist. After returning to India, on her mother's insistence she took part in the Femina Miss India contents and by the grace of God she was selected as the Miss World representing India. She was crowned Miss World 2000 in the Pageant. And, thereafter her journey as in the film industry began where she later on made a big name for herself. She began her career with Tamil film named 'Thamizan' and then she made her debut in the Bollywood as a second lead in the movie Andaaz. And, she was also awarded Filmfare Award for her role in Andaaz and that gave her a great start getting her great movies like Krrish, Aitraaz, Fashion, 7 Khoon Maaf, Barfi and many more. By giving great performance in these challenging roles she has been awarded with a number of titles and had also achieved recognition as an International actress, singer and a performer.
